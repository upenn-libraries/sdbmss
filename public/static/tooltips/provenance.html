Provenance

Enter provenance from earliest to latest. Name of institution or individual only — do not include "library of", "collection of", "ex libris", etc. Use naming standards found in SELLER 2 and INSTITUTION. In general, personal names should be entered last name first. Include all dealers in possession of manuscripts. Do not enter auction houses.
e.g.:    Phillipps, Thomas, Sir
  Quaritch
  Schoenberg, Lawrence J.
  Charles V, King of France
  Anne of Brittany, Consort of Louis XII, King of France
  Aulne Abbey
  Eton College, Cambridge University

Note: Render all accented letters as the equivalent without diacritical marks. e.g., à / á / â ⇒ a, æ ⇒ ae, ä ⇒ ae, ç ⇒ c, è / é / ê ⇒ e, ì / í / î ⇒ i, œ ⇒ oe, ö ⇒ oe, ü ⇒ ue, etc.
