Seller 2

Enter name of private collector or institution selling manuscripts through the auction house or dealer as recorded in SELLER1. For individuals, enter last name first.
e.g.:    Schoenberg, Lawrence J.

If an institution has changed names over the course of time, give the name at the time of transaction as recorded in the source.
e.g.:    Walters Art Gallery        before 2001
  Walters Art Museum  after 2001

Personal names including von, van, de, di, etc., should be written as follows:
e.g.:    Ess, Leander van

For names of nobility, the personal name is given first (last name first), then the title:
e.g.:    Ashburnham, Bertram, 4th Earl of Ashburnham
  Ligerolles, M. le comte de

If only the title of a member of nobility is given in the source, enter as follows:
e.g.:    Berry, Duchesse de

Military ranks or other honorifics should be given last:
e.g.:    Abbey, John Roland, Major
  Phillipps, Thomas, Sir

Note: Render all accented letters as the equivalent without diacritical marks. e.g., à / á / â ⇒ a, æ ⇒ ae, ä ⇒ ae, ç ⇒ c, è / é / ê ⇒ e, ì / í / î ⇒ i, œ ⇒ oe, ö ⇒ oe, ü ⇒ ue, etc.
