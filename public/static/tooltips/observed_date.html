Date As Recorded

  • Enter the date that the manuscript was created, exactly as given in the data source.
  • If the date is A.D., just enter the year. If a date is B.C., enter the year with a minus sign before it (e.g., -300 for 300 B.C.).
  • You may also enter approximate dates in this field, ex: "early 13th century".
  • Dates in other calendar systems are acceptable.
