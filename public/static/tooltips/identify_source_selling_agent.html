Selling Agent or Institution/Collection

Enter name of auction house, dealer, collector, collection, etc as it appears in the source or as it was known at the time of the transaction. If an individual dealer or seller’s firm is identified by name, give the legal name of the firm. If a private individual seller, give last name first. If a donation, enter the individual’s name, using the SDBM Personal Name Standard:

Last name, First Name, Title

Do not use commas between names and hometowns or between names and common descriptors. Spell out common abbreviations of given names.

e.g.:
Sotheby's
Sotheby’s NYC
British Library
Leo S. Olschki
Lewis, John Frederick
