Year

Enter the year that the manuscript was created. If the date is A.D., just enter the year. If a date is B.C., enter the year with a minus sign before it (e.g., -300 for 300 B.C.).

If date is given in century form, use the following formula: 15th century = 1450, 14th century = 1350; 13th century = 1250, etc. It is then neccessary to add appropriate notation in CIRCA, quod vide.
