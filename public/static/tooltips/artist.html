Artist

Enter name of artist or artists. Use the Union List of Artists Names (ULAN) to find the standard name. If an individual with a known name, give the last name first.
e.g.: Pucelle, Jean
De Brailes, William

When schools, circles, workshops, etc. associated with a named artist or master are given, give the name of the artist first, then the appropriate qualifier:
e.g.:      Bening, Simon, follower
    Master of Edward IV, workshop
    Master of the White Inscriptions
    Coetivy Master, follower (Henri de Vulcop?)

In cases where an accepted name of an anonymous master has changed from what is given in the catalogue, put the former name in parentheses after the current name.
e.g.:      Master B.F. (Hippolyta Master)    ⇐    [ now "Master B.F." + was "Hippolyta Master" ]

Note: Render all accented letters as the equivalent without diacritical marks. e.g., à / á / â ⇒ a, æ ⇒ ae, ä ⇒ ae, ç ⇒ c, è / é / ê ⇒ e, ì / í / î ⇒ i, œ ⇒ oe, ö ⇒ oe, ü ⇒ ue, etc.
