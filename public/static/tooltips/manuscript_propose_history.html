This tool allows you to suggest a provenance history for this manuscript record by creating a new personal observation based on the provenance data aggregated in the manuscript record. Click and drag individual provenance agents from the Entry Provenance column into the Proposed History column. Arrange provenance agents in chronological order, beginning with the earliest. Reorder the provenance agents within the Proposed History by clicking on an agent and dragging it to the appropriate position in the list. To add manuscript details once the new entry is created, click Edit in the Tools window.
