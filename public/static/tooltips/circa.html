Circa

Enter the descriptive code to qualify approximate date given in YEAR. Use the following codes: C (circa), CCENT (circa a century), C? (very uncertain circa), C1H (circa first half of century), C2H (circa second half of century), C1Q (circa first quarter of century), C2Q (circa second quarter of century), C3Q (circa third quarter of century), C4Q (circa fourth quarter of century), CEARLY (circa early part of century), CLATE (circa late part of century), CMID (circa middle of century).

e.g.:    "15th century"   ⇒   YEAR = 1450     CIRCA = CCENT
  "Early 15th century"   ⇒   YEAR = 1450  CIRCA = CEARLY
  "First half of the 15th century"   ⇒   YEAR = 1450  CIRCA = C1H
  "Late 14th-early 15th" or "c. 1500"   ⇒   YEAR = 1500  CIRCA = C
  "ca. 1400"   ⇒   YEAR = 1400  CIRCA = C
