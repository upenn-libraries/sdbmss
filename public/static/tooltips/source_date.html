Source Date

Enter date in YYYY-MM-DD, YYYY-MM, or YYYY format in Auction/Sale Catalog, Collection Catalog, Published Source, Personal Observation, or Other Published Source. For multi-day auctions, enter the first day of the auction. For Online-only Auction or Bookseller Websites, enter the date you (the data entry person) viewed the data. For sources with no date, leave the date field blank. This is not a required field.
