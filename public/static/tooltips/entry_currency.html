Currency

Use this dropdown menu to enter the standard, 3-letter abbreviation for the most common currencies. These follow the ISO 4217 abbreviation standard.

EUR = European Union Euro
GBP = British Pound
USD = United States Dollar
CHF = Swiss Franc
FRF = French Franc
BEF = Belgian Franc
DEM = Deutsche Mark
ITL = Italian Lira
DKK = Danish Krone
JPY = Japanese Yen
